Welcome to The Draft Room

Hi there, I'm Priscy Olad.

I am a passionate screenwriter dedicated to bringing compelling characters, sharp dialogue, and gripping narratives to the page.

The Draft Room is my personal portfolio and creative space—a place where my original scripts live before they hit the screen. Whether you're an industry professional looking for fresh voices, a fellow writer, or just someone who loves a great story, you are in the right place.

Storytelling is a collaborative art. I invite you to dive into my latest screenplays, explore the worlds I've built, and drop a review to let me know your thoughts.
